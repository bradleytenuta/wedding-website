Our Story

Our story began long before we ever realised it would lead us here. We first met in nursery, growing up around each other without knowing that one day we would build a life together.

Over the years our friendship grew into something more, and we’ve been by each other’s side ever since. From supporting each other through university to building our careers, we’ve always been each other’s biggest supporters.

We love exploring new places together, but we’re just as happy enjoying the simple moments long walks, gigs and quiet evenings watching movies.

In 2023 we bought our first home together, a place that truly felt like the beginning of our future. Then in 2024, during a special Christmas visit to Castle Howard, we got engaged.

After sharing so much of life already, we feel incredibly lucky to begin this next chapter together and celebrate it with the people we love most.
